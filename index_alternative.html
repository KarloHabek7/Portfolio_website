About Me

I am a third-year student at the Faculty of Mechanical Engineering and Naval Architecture (FSB / FAMENA) in Zagreb, specializing in mechatronics and robotics. Alongside my studies, I work as a teaching assistant for the courses Mathematics 1 and Mathematics 2.

Development projects are not only obligations for me but also a hobby. I feel particularly satisfied when a project has a certain level of complexity, and its completion brings real progress.

I strive for perfection in results and optimization of the work process, but I have no problem adapting to real conditions if they do not allow for it.
